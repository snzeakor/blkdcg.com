// FAQ

Got
Questions?

Answers to the questions we hear most. If you don't see yours here, we're always a call away.

Call Now
Do you work directly with insurance companies?
+
Yes — we specialize in insurance claim management. We inspect your property, prepare a full damage report, and work directly with your adjuster. We handle the paperwork so you don't have to.
How fast do you respond after a storm?
+
Our emergency response team can typically be on-site within 24–48 hours. We prioritize emergency tarping and temporary repairs to prevent further water intrusion while the restoration plan is finalized.
Are you licensed and insured in Texas?
+
Absolutely. Black Diamond holds all required Texas contractor licenses across our trades — roofing, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, and general contracting. All team members carry full liability and workers' comp coverage.
Do you handle commercial work too?
+
Yes. We serve both homeowners and business owners. Commercial services include retail remodels, office buildouts, commercial roofing, and full multi-trade construction management.
What warranty do you offer?
+
Written workmanship warranties on all labor. Roofing systems carry manufacturer warranties from 25 years to lifetime, depending on the product. All terms are included in your contract.
Are estimates really free?
+
100% free, no obligation. We visit your property, assess scope, and deliver a detailed itemized quote within 1–2 business days. Zero pressure, ever.
What areas of DFW do you cover?
+
We cover the entire Dallas–Fort Worth Metropolitan Area — all major cities across all 11 DFW counties. If you're in the metroplex, we come to you.
